**Course Content: HTML**

1. **Document structure :** <!DOCTYPE>, <html>, <head>, <body>,Content language, charset, viewport
2. **Metadata :** <title>, <meta>, favicons, linking CSS/JS
3. **Semantic HTML :** <header>, <nav>, <main>, <article>, <section>, <aside>, <footer> <div> <span> 
4. **Attributes**
5. **Text basics :** <p>, <h1>-<h6>, <title>,<strong>, <em>, <br>, <hr>, 
6. **Links :** <a href="">, target, relative and absolute lin**k**
7. **Lists :** <ul>, <ol>, <li>
8. **Navigation**
9. **Tables**
10. **Forms :** <form>, <input> types (text, email, password, checkbox, radio, submit, number), <label>, <textarea>, <select>/<option>, name attribute, placeholder
11. **Images :** <img src="" alt="">
12. **Audio and Video :** <video>, <audio>, <figure>, <figcaption>
13. **Template, slot, and shadow**
